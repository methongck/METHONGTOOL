About Our Age Calculator

Our free online Age Calculator is a simple yet powerful tool that accurately determines a person's age in years, months, and days based on their date of birth and the current date. This tool is useful for various purposes, from quickly finding out someone's exact age to calculating age for legal, educational, or medical requirements. It provides precise results, taking into account leap years and varying month lengths.

How to Use the Age Calculator

  1. Enter Date of Birth: Click on the date input field and select the birth date from the calendar.
  2. Click "Calculate Age": The tool will instantly calculate and display the age in years, months, and days.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How accurate is the age calculation?
Our calculator provides a precise age calculation down to the day, taking into account all calendar intricacies, including leap years.
Can I calculate age for a future date?
The calculator is designed to calculate age up to the current date. For future age calculations, you would need a different type of date calculator.
Is my date of birth stored?
No, all calculations are performed directly in your web browser. Your date of birth is not stored or transmitted to our servers, ensuring your privacy.
