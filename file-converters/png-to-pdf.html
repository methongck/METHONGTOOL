About Our PNG to PDF Converter

Our PNG to PDF converter is a free online tool that allows you to easily convert your PNG images into a single, professional-quality PDF document. This is incredibly useful for a variety of purposes, such as creating reports, presentations, or simply archiving your images in a universally accessible format. Our tool is fast, easy to use, and respects your privacy by processing all files in your browser.

How to Use the PNG to PDF Converter

  1. Upload Your PNG Images: You can either drag and drop your PNG files into the designated area or click the "Browse Files" button to select them from your computer. You can upload multiple images at once.
  2. Set PDF Dimensions (Optional): You can specify the width and height of the output PDF. The default is a standard A4 size.
  3. Click "Convert to PDF": The tool will process your images and combine them into a single PDF document.
  4. Preview and Download Your PDF: Once the conversion is complete, a preview of the PDF will be displayed. You can then download the PDF to your computer.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why should I convert my PNG images to PDF?
Converting PNGs to PDF is a great way to bundle multiple images into a single file for easy sharing and printing. PDFs are also a standard format for official documents and reports.
Can I convert multiple PNGs at once?
Yes, you can upload multiple PNG images, and our tool will combine them into a single PDF document, with each image on a separate page.
Is it safe to use this online converter?
Yes, our tool processes your images in your browser. Your files are not uploaded to our servers, so your privacy is protected.
