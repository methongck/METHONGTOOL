About Our PNG to WEBP Converter

Our PNG to WEBP converter is a free online tool that allows you to convert your PNG images to the modern and efficient WEBP format. WEBP is a next-generation image format developed by Google that provides superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web. Converting your PNGs to WEBP can result in significantly smaller file sizes without a noticeable loss in quality, leading to faster website load times and a better user experience.

How to Use the PNG to WEBP Converter

  1. Upload Your PNG Images: You can either drag and drop your PNG files into the designated area or click the "Browse Files" button to select them from your computer.
  2. Adjust WEBP Quality (Optional): You can adjust the quality of the output WEBP file. A lower quality will result in a smaller file size, while a higher quality will preserve more detail.
  3. Click "Convert to WEBP": The tool will process your images and convert them to the WEBP format.
  4. Download Your WEBP Images: Once the conversion is complete, you can download your new WEBP files individually or as a single ZIP file.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is WEBP?
WEBP is an image format developed by Google that offers both lossy and lossless compression. It is designed to create smaller, richer images that make the web faster.
Why should I use WEBP instead of PNG?
WEBP images are typically much smaller than PNGs, which can significantly improve your website's performance. WEBP also supports transparency, just like PNG.
Is WEBP supported by all browsers?
WEBP is now supported by all major modern browsers, including Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari.
