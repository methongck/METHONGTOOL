About Our QR Code Decoder

Our free online QR Code Decoder allows you to quickly extract the information embedded within any QR code image. QR codes are two-dimensional barcodes that can store various types of data, such as website URLs, text, contact information, or Wi-Fi credentials. This tool is useful for anyone who needs to access the content of a QR code without a smartphone app, or for developers testing QR code generation.

How to Use the QR Code Decoder

  1. Upload QR Code Image: Drag and drop your QR code image file into the designated area, or click the "Browse File" button to select it from your computer.
  2. Decode QR Code: Click "Decode QR Code" to process the image.
  3. View Decoded Information: The decoded text or URL will be displayed in the result box.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a QR Code?
A QR (Quick Response) Code is a two-dimensional barcode that can be read by smartphones and other devices. It can store a variety of information, including URLs, text, and contact details.
What types of information can a QR Code store?
QR codes can store website URLs, plain text, email addresses, phone numbers, SMS messages, geographic coordinates, Wi-Fi network credentials, and more.
Is my uploaded image stored?
No, all image processing and QR code decoding are performed directly in your web browser. Your image files are not uploaded to our servers, ensuring your privacy and data security.
