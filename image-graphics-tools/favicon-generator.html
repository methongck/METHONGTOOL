About Our Favicon Generator

Our free online Favicon Generator allows you to easily create favicons for your website from any image. A favicon (favorite icon) is a small icon displayed in the web browser's address bar, browser tabs, and bookmarks. It helps in branding your website and makes it easily recognizable to users. Our tool generates favicons in various sizes, ensuring compatibility across different browsers and devices.

How to Use the Favicon Generator

  1. Upload Your Image: Drag and drop your image file into the designated area, or click the "Browse File" button to select it from your computer.
  2. Generate Favicons: Click "Generate Favicons" to create favicons in multiple standard sizes.
  3. Download Favicons: Once generated, you can download individual favicon files or a complete package for easy implementation on your website.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a favicon?
A favicon is a small icon associated with a particular website, typically displayed in the address bar of a web browser, next to the site's name in a list of bookmarks, and in browser tabs.
Why is a favicon important?
Favicons enhance user experience by making your website easily identifiable in browser tabs and bookmarks. They contribute to your brand's visual identity and professionalism.
What image formats are supported for input?
Our tool supports common image formats like JPG, PNG, and WEBP for generating favicons.
